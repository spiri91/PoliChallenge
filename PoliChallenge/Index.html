Game play

Wellcome to PoliChallenge

This is a fun game that will allow you and your friends to know the Politehnica Park a bit better.

How is it played?

First, you must have the location active on your device, and find the main entrance to Politehnica Park from Iuliu Maniu street. You can see in the bottom right corner how far are you from the destination. Once you are in close range to your destination (20m or less), you will be guided to the first faculty that you will have to find. Don't rush, enjoy the day and start finding it. Once you are close to it, a game quiz will pop up with questions related to the profile of that faculty, your goal is to answer as many as you can, once you have 2 answers wrong you will be guided to other faculty and so on, until you visit everything that is to see in the campus or you get bored.

You can see how well your team did on the HiScore link above.


Also, feel free to contact me anytime with suggestions or possible bugs.
